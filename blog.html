How to Test 22k Gold at Home

Testing Guide Methods

Learn safe and effective home methods to test 22k gold authenticity.

Simple Gold Testing Methods

1. Magnet Test: Real gold is not magnetic. Use a strong magnet to test.

2. Weight & Density Test: 22k gold has specific weight characteristics.

3. Sound Test: Gold produces a distinct sound when tapped.

4. Float Test: Pure gold sinks in water, fakes may float.

5. Color & Shine: Check for consistent color and authentic shine.
